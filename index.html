Your data & privacy

VeraFuel records the food, training, sleep and body data you choose to log, so the app can calculate your targets and show your progress.

What we store: your entries are saved to your own device and securely synced so you don't lose them. Nothing is shared with other users.

During this beta: the VeraFuel team may review anonymised, aggregated data to fix bugs and improve the app — including new foods you add, which may be added to the shared food library to benefit everyone.

Your control: the data is yours. You can ask us to delete it at any time, and you can stop using the app whenever you like.

This is health-related information, and we treat it with care and in line with data-protection law.
